## Rules

We use the current [WRC 2025 Rules](https://static1.squarespace.com/static/634a7884c297a25f06589b79/t/6834d67360e19c1da6c0d12c/1748293243651/WRC+Rules+2025.pdf), with the following modifications.
- Red dora is used, one per suit;
- No kazoe yakuman (i.e. 13+ han is counted as sanbaiman);
- Uma is +30/+10/-10/-30;
- Timer is 60+1 with autotable, and 75+0 without autotable.

## Quick References
Below are some quick references to the WRC rules already in place (but you might find them surprising).

**General**
- **No** abortive draw of any kind.
- No more than four kan's can be made.
- OK to riichi even with less than 4 tiles remain in the wall.
- No agari yame.
   - i.e. the game **does not** automatically end if the all-last hand ends with the dealer in 1st place and in tenpai.
- There is liability payment for daisangen, daisuushii and suukantsu.
  - In case of multiple yakuman, only pay for the one you're responsible for.
- No West round.

**Scoring/Yaku**
- Riichi deposits at the end of a hanchan will not be awarded to anyone - it will be recorded as such.
- No double yakuman from a single yaku.
  - e.g. daisuushii is 1x yakuman, but daisuushii + tsuuiisou is 2x yakuman.
- **No** nagashi mangan.
- Koukushi **cannot** be won by robbing a concealed kan.
- Kiriage mangan (4 han 30/3 han 60 rounds up to mangan).
- There is renhou.
  - One declares Ron with their starting hand, without drawing or anyone calling.
  - It is worth 5 han (but does not combine with other yaku or dora). Riichi decl. does not break renhou.
- Double wind pair is worth 2 fu.
